ABOUT NOOTAN MEDICAL COLLEGE AND RESEARCH CENTRE & NOOTAN GENERAL HOSPITAL

Nootan Medical College and Research Centre was established with the objective to provide adequate medical facilities to the people of North Gujarat. The college is fully equipped with hi-tech machinery, latest infrastructural facilities, highly qualified and experienced teaching and paramedical staff necessary to run the medical college. The Nootan General Hospital was established in 2006 consisting of 200 beds with the objective to provide medical facilities to people of North Gujarat. In 2015 the hospital was renovated and extended to 300 beds with basic medical facilities to meet the medical needs of nearby regions.
