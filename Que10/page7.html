ABOUT NOOTAN PHARMACY COLLEGE

The Nootan Pharmacy College (NPC) is a pioneering institute in pharmaceutical education in India and was founded in 2006. The college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and Pharmacy Council of India (PCI). It currently offers Bachelor’s, Master’s and Doctoral programs in Pharmaceutical Science. NPC has produced more than 500 Pharmacists, 100 M. Pharm and 17 Ph.D. Graduates. Many of the NPC alumni now hold prominent positions in the industry and academia both in India and abroad. NPC is has well equipped laboratories with some of the most ultra-modern instruments and facilities for drug testing and training.

NPC has always encouraged a collaborative atmosphere and regularly undertakes collaborative projects with industries and academia. NPC has successfully completed more than 14 industry sponsored projects as of now. The institute keeps organizing workshops, conferences and seminars for pharmacy faculties, PG students and research scholars at regular intervals.
