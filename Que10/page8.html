ABOUT NOOTAN COLLEGE OF NURSING

Nootan College of Nursing, a constituent institute of Sankalchand Patel University, was established in 2011 to promote nursing education which is a crucial part in the health care delivery system. The program is designed to aid in the progress of research in nursing. Nootan College of Nursing is recognized as one of the best nursing colleges in Gujarat, and is approved by Indian Nursing Council and Gujarat Nursing Council.
